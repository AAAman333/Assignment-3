Tribute to Hello Kitty

About Hello Kitty

Since 1974

Hello Kitty is a fictional character created by the Japanese company Sanrio. She is a sweet little girl with a red bow and no mouth, symbolizing kindness and friendship. Since her debut in 1974, Hello Kitty has become one of the most beloved and iconic characters worldwide.

Major Milestones

  1. 1974 – First appearance on a vinyl coin purse in Japan.
  2. 1976 – Hello Kitty trademark registered in the United States.
  3. 1980s – Global expansion with toys, clothes, and accessories.
  4. 2004 – Celebrated 30th anniversary with worldwide events.
  5. Today – Recognized as a global pop culture icon.

Famous Merchandise & Impact

  • Stationery and school supplies
  • Toys and plush dolls
  • Clothing and fashion accessories
  • Collaborations with global brands
